# Support & Legal

## Data Deletion Request

To request the permanent deletion of your personal data and profile from the Healthterra app, please send an email to **kostasvrou12@gmail.com**. In your email, you **must** include your unique **Account ID** (this can be found at the bottom of your Profile screen inside the app).

Once we receive your request with the correct Account ID, your data will be permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days.
